# Logo Design Prompt for xp7k

## Service Description
A Telegram Mini App platform that combines:
- **Token/Cryptocurrency Swapping**: Exchange and trade tokens seamlessly
- **AI-Powered Q&A**: Intelligent assistant for crypto questions and market insights
- **TON Blockchain Integration**: Native support for TON ecosystem
- **Real-time Charts**: Price data visualization and market trends
- **Wallet Connectivity**: Secure wallet integration via TON Connect

## Logo Design Prompt

**Create a modern, minimalist logo for a Telegram Mini App that combines cryptocurrency token swapping with AI intelligence. The logo should:**

### Visual Elements:
- **Primary Symbol**: A stylized combination of:
  - Two circular tokens/coins in a swap/exchange motion (arrows or rotation between them)
  - Integrated AI/neural network pattern (subtle nodes or connection lines)
  - Or: A single coin/token with a brain/neural network overlay
  - Or: An abstract "X" or "7" shape (from "xp7k") formed by intersecting swap arrows

### Style & Aesthetics:
- **Modern & Minimalist**: Clean, simple design that works at small sizes (Telegram app icon)
- **Tech-forward**: Sleek, digital aesthetic suggesting AI and blockchain technology
- **Bold & Confident**: Strong visual presence that conveys trust and innovation
- **Monochrome-friendly**: Works in both light and dark themes (black/white variations)
- **Gradient option**: Subtle gradient suggesting digital/tech (blue-purple or orange-blue)

### Color Palette Options:
1. **Primary**: Deep blue (#007AFF) or vibrant purple (#6C5CE7) - tech/blockchain feel
2. **Accent**: Electric blue or cyan for AI/intelligence
3. **Alternative**: Orange/amber gradient suggesting energy and trading activity
4. **Monochrome**: Black/white for versatility

### Technical Requirements:
- **Scalable**: Vector-based, works from 16x16px to 512x512px
- **Readable**: Clear and recognizable even at Telegram's smallest icon size
- **Versatile**: Works on both light and dark backgrounds
- **Simple**: Not overly complex - should be memorable and instantly recognizable

### Concept Variations:

**Option 1: Swap + AI**
- Two overlapping circular tokens with swap arrows
- Neural network pattern subtly integrated in the background or as texture
- Modern, geometric style

**Option 2: Token with Brain**
- Single coin/token icon
- Brain or neural network pattern visible through/on the token
- Suggests intelligent trading

**Option 3: Abstract "X" or "7"**
- Stylized "X" or "7" (from "xp7k") made of:
  - Intersecting swap arrows
  - Or: Token shapes forming the letter
  - Or: Neural network nodes connected in letter shape

**Option 4: Minimalist Coin Stack**
- Two or three coins/tokens stacked with a subtle AI pattern
- Clean, professional look
- Suggests both trading and intelligence

### Mood & Personality:
- **Smart**: Conveys intelligence and AI capabilities
- **Fast**: Suggests quick swaps and real-time data
- **Trustworthy**: Professional, secure appearance
- **Innovative**: Cutting-edge, forward-thinking design
- **Accessible**: Friendly, not intimidating (Telegram audience)

### Usage Context:
- Telegram Mini App icon (primary use case)
- App header/logo
- Marketing materials
- Social media profiles
- Website favicon
